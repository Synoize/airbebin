FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to deliver a pre-built application or website?

Typically, a prebuilt application takes 7–14 working days to customize and deploy to your domain after receiving all required assets and credentials from you.

Do you provide the complete source code after development?

Yes! For custom development projects, complete source code is handed over after final payment. Prebuilt solutions are provided as white-label deployments.

What's the difference between a pre-built and a custom development project?

Prebuilt solutions are ready-made, whitelabeled products (like our grocery app) deployed for your brand quickly and affordably. Custom development is built from scratch to match your specific unique requirements.

Do you provide maintenance and support after delivery?

Yes, we offer flexible maintenance plans. All projects include a free 30-day post-launch support window. Extended plans are available monthly or annually.

What technologies do you use for development?

We use Flutter for mobile (Android & iOS), React/Next.js for web frontends, Node.js and Firebase for backends, and Figma for design. We pick the right stack for each project's needs.
