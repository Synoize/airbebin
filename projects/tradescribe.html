// features

Everything a serious trader needs

📓

Trade Journal

Log entries, exits, setup type, emotions, and screenshots. Build a searchable database of every trade you've ever made.

🤖

AI Pattern Detection

Our AI identifies behavioural patterns — overtrading on Mondays, weak performance after losses — and coaches you to fix them.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Win rate by setup, time of day, instrument, and more. Customizable P&L charts and drawdown analysis.

🔔

Rule Alerts

Set trading rules and get notified when you break them. Build discipline through accountability.

📤

Broker Sync

Auto-import trades from Zerodha, Groww, Angel One via API or CSV. No manual entry required.

🏆

Leaderboard

Optionally share your performance and compete with community traders. Anonymous mode available.
