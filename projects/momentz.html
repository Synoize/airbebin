The Brief

Gifting should feel as special as the moment itself

Momentz curates luxury gifts and artisanal home décor. The challenge: how do you communicate warmth, elegance, and personal connection through a digital storefront?

We built an experience-led store — with occasion-based discovery, gift personalization tools, message card editor, and premium packaging visualization — that makes buying a gift feel like giving one.

🎀
Gift Personalization Studio
Add message cards, choose wrapping, select ribbon colour — all visualized in real-time before checkout.
📅
Occasion Reminders
Never miss an anniversary or birthday — save dates and get automated reminders 7 days before with curated suggestions.
🏢
Corporate Gifting Portal
Bulk order portal for corporate clients with branded packaging, invoice management, and delivery tracking.
🚀
Same-day Delivery
Real-time slot booking for same-day and next-day delivery with live order tracking via WhatsApp.
