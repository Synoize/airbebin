The Brief

Fashion deserves a stage, not just a storefront

Murzban's physical stores had atmosphere — scent, texture, the weight of fine fabric. We had to translate that sensory richness into pixels and interactions.

The result: a dark, editorial-first store with editorial lookbooks, zoomable fabric close-ups, size guide overlays, and a checkout that feels like stepping into the brand's world.

Key Features
Lookbook Collections
Seasonal editorial shoots integrated into shoppable product pages.
Smart Size Recommender
AI-driven fit suggestions based on body measurements, reducing returns by 40%.
Wishlist & Styling
Curate personal collections and share complete outfit looks with friends.
One-Page Checkout
Streamlined single-page flow with address autofill and saved payment methods.
