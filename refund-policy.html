Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: April 2026

⚠️ Please read this policy carefully before making any payment. By purchasing our services, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Overview

We provide digital services including but not limited to website development, application development, UI/UX design, paid advertising, social media management, and business automation. Due to the intangible and customized nature of these services, refunds are limited and governed strictly by this policy.

Our goal is to ensure transparency, fairness, and clarity between both parties before, during, and after project execution.

2. Non-Refundable Services

Once a project has been confirmed and work has commenced, all payments made are strictly non-refundable. This includes (but is not limited to):

  • Website or application development
  • UI/UX design and creative design services
  • Paid advertising campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Ads, etc.)
  • Social media management and content creation
  • Business automation setup and integrations

This policy exists because resources, time, tools, and manpower are allocated immediately after project initiation.

3. Advance Payments

All advance payments (including booking fees or initial deposits) are non-refundable under any circumstances. These payments are used for:

  • Project planning and consultation
  • Resource allocation and onboarding
  • Initial research and setup work

4. Cancellation Policy

Clients may request project cancellation; however, refunds are subject to the stage of the project:

  • If cancellation is requested before work begins, a partial refund may be issued after deducting administrative and consultation charges.
  • If cancellation is requested after work has started, no refund will be provided.

5. Revisions Instead of Refunds

We prioritize client satisfaction and offer revisions based on the agreed project scope. Instead of refunds, clients are encouraged to request revisions to improve the final outcome.

Any additional work outside the original scope may incur extra charges.

6. Third-Party Services & Costs

We are not responsible for refunds related to third-party services. These include:

  • Domain registration and hosting services
  • Paid advertising budgets (Google Ads, Meta Ads, etc.)
  • Software subscriptions, plugins, or APIs

Such payments are made directly to third-party providers and are governed by their respective refund policies.

7. Delays & Client Responsibility

Delays caused by the client (such as lack of communication, missing content, or delayed approvals) do not qualify for refunds. Project timelines may be adjusted accordingly.

8. Service-Based Results Disclaimer

For marketing and advertising services, results such as leads, conversions, or revenue cannot be guaranteed. Performance depends on multiple external factors including market conditions, competition, and audience behavior.

Therefore, refunds will not be issued based on performance expectations.

9. Exceptional Cases

Refund requests under exceptional circumstances will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Approval of such requests is solely at our discretion and will depend on the situation and work completed.

10. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time without prior notice. Updated versions will be posted on this page with the revised date.
